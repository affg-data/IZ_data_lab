# IZ Data Lab

## Overview

**IZ Data Lab** is a collection of data analysis and visualization projects exploring macroeconomics, financial markets, and cryptocurrency using publicly available datasets.

The goal of this repository is to transform complex economic and financial data into clear, informative visualizations that help explain long-term trends, market relationships, and historical events.

The projects combine data from government agencies, central banks, and industry sources with Python-based analysis and visualization.

---

## Projects

### ₿ Bitcoin vs. Stablecoin Liquidity

Examines crypto market liquidity using:

- Bitcoin Price
- Stablecoin Market Capitalization
- Stablecoin Supply Ratio (SSR)
- Liquidity Ratio

Focus:
- Crypto liquidity
- Stablecoin growth
- Market cycles
- Capital availability

---

### ₿ Bitcoin vs. U.S. 10-Year Real Yield (TIPS)

Explores the relationship between Bitcoin and U.S. inflation-adjusted Treasury yields.

Focus:
- Real interest rates
- Opportunity cost
- Federal Reserve policy
- Rolling correlation analysis

---

### 🌍 Strait of Hormuz: Global Oil Trade

Visualizes crude oil flows through one of the world's most strategically important energy chokepoints.

Focus:
- Global oil trade
- Persian Gulf exports
- Asian energy demand
- Energy security
- Geopolitics

---

### 📈 Phillips Curve Time Series Analysis

Explores the historical relationship between inflation and unemployment using three different inflation measures.

Series include:

1. Headline CPI vs. Unemployment
2. Core CPI vs. Unemployment
3. Core PCE vs. Unemployment

Focus:
- Inflation
- Labor markets
- Monetary policy
- Business cycles

---

### 📊 U.S. Inflation Decomposition

Breaks down U.S. inflation into its major components while incorporating unemployment, monetary policy, recessions, and historical oil shocks.

Focus:
- Headline CPI
- Core CPI
- Food inflation
- Energy inflation
- Federal Funds Rate
- Recessions
- Oil shocks

## Purpose

The purpose of **IZ Data Lab** is to build a collection of reproducible, data-driven analyses that explain important economic, financial, and geopolitical topics through visualization.

Rather than focusing solely on statistical models, these projects emphasize historical context, market dynamics, and intuitive storytelling to make complex data more accessible.

---

## About

IZ Data Lab is an evolving collection of analyses. New projects are added as they are completed, with a focus on creating clear, reproducible visualizations that help explain real-world economic and financial trends.
